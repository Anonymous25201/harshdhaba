Welcome to Harsh Dhaba!

Harsh Dhaba and Restaurant has been proudly serving delicious, home-style Indian food to travelers and locals alike in Khairthal. We believe in the simple joy of good food – fresh ingredients, generous portions, and a welcoming atmosphere.

From sizzling tandoori starters to hearty curries and freshly baked breads, every dish is prepared with care, bringing you the true taste of India.
