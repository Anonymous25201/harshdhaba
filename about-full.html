Our Journey & Values

The Beginning of Harsh Dhaba

Harsh Dhaba and Restaurant started with a simple vision: to provide delicious, authentic Indian food in a warm and welcoming environment for everyone passing through Khairthal on Kishangarh Road. Founded by Mr. Harish Kumar Babani S/o Mr. Vasudev Babani. We wanted to create a space that felt like a home away from home, serving dishes made with love and the freshest local ingredients.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown thanks to the support of our loyal customers and our commitment to quality. We remember the early days, serving chai and parathas to truck drivers and local families, and those core values of hospitality and good food remain with us today.

Our Philosophy: Fresh Food, Warm Hearts

At Harsh Dhaba, we believe that great food starts with great ingredients. We source our produce locally whenever possible, ensuring freshness and supporting our community. Our spices are hand-picked and ground to create the authentic flavors that our guests have come to love.

More than just food, we aim to offer an experience. Whether you're stopping for a quick meal on your journey or celebrating a special occasion with family, we want you to feel comfortable and cared for. Our team is dedicated to providing friendly service and making your visit memorable.

Our Commitment to Khairthal

Being located in Khairthal, Rajasthan, opposite Galaxy Green City on Kishangarh Road, we are proud to be a part of this vibrant community. We strive to be more than just a restaurant; we aim to be a landmark where good food and good company meet. We are grateful for the continued support from our neighbours and visitors.
